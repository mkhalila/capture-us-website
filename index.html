FAQs


Is there a cost for delivery?

We provide a FREE delivery within a return trip of 30 miles from East London. If you are further than 30 miles please contact us and we can let you know how much the charge will be.

Can I get a refund for the time the booth is not being used?

No refund will be given if guests choose not to use all of the time rented from. For example, if your rental is from 1pm-4pm yet the party ends early at 3pm and you instruct our attendant to leave early, you will not be eligible for a refund of the 1 hour that did not get used.

How long does it take to set-up a booth?

Your booth attendant will usually arrive at your venue at least 1 -2 hours before the event or hire is booked to begin and a typical set-up time would be around 30-45 minutes. We do not charge for this time and it will not be counted as part of your running time. All we require is a plug electrical socket and space of around 5 m2.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

We will require a £100 deposit (non-refundable) to confirm and secure your booking which can be paid for at the time of the booking. The balance for your booth will only be required 30 days before your actual event.

Are you insured?

We carry pubic liability insurance and we can send the certificates to your venue.

Will we get a copy of the photos?

Yes. We will provide you with a unique and private link to your photos. Simply visit the link to view and download your photos.
